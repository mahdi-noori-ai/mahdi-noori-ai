## 👋 About Me

I'm **Mahdi Noori**, a **Computer Engineering graduate, 2026**, focused on building scalable backend systems, AI-powered applications, and FinTech/quant-driven products.

I work as a **Developer at ITRC — Iran Telecommunication Research Center**, where I contribute to R&D projects around home IoT, automation, and security-testing workflows. My work combines **Python backend engineering**, **machine learning**, **NLP**, and practical product development.

My main focus right now is building **AI microservices** — turning machine learning models into clean, reliable, deployable APIs — while going deeper into **quantitative finance, algorithmic trading, market data pipelines, and sentiment-driven trading tools**.

I care about building software that is not just technically interesting, but actually useful, scalable, and valuable.
